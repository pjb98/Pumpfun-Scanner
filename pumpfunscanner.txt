The most useful pre-migration metrics
1. Bonding speed

This may be one of the best signals.

Track:

Time to 25% bonded
Time to 50% bonded
Time to 75% bonded
Time to 90% bonded

Fast example:

0% → 50% bonded in 4 minutes
50% → 75% bonded in 3 minutes

That tells you the token has momentum.

Bad example:

0% → 40% bonded in 3 minutes
40% → 42% bonded over 20 minutes

That is probably stalling.

2. Curve acceleration

Don’t just track current bonding percent. Track whether it is speeding up or slowing down.

Bonding acceleration = current 5m bonding speed - previous 5m bonding speed

Example:

Time	Bonded %
12:00	22%
12:05	38%
12:10	63%

That is accelerating.

But:

Time	Bonded %
12:00	22%
12:05	45%
12:10	48%

That is dying.

This could become a simple label:

Curve Status: Accelerating / Slowing / Stalled / Reversing
3. Entry zone tracker

You could backtest which curve zones are best for you.

Example zones:

Zone	Meaning
0–20% bonded	very early, highest risk
20–40%	early momentum
40–65%	possible sweet spot
65–85%	crowded but stronger confirmation
85–100%	migration chase risk

Your dashboard should answer:

When buying tokens between 35–60% bonded:
- 2x hit rate: X%
- average drawdown: X%
- rug/dev dump rate: X%
- average hold time: X minutes

For your style, I’d care more about:

Best pre-migration entry zone by expected value

not just “which tokens migrated.”

A token can look dangerous early but get healthier as buyers come in.

Track:

Bad signal:

Curve rising but top 10 concentration staying high = dump risk

10. Stalled curve detector

This is very important for pre-migration trading.

Alert when:

Bonding progress has moved less than 3% in 10 minutes
AND sell volume is increasing
AND unique buyers are flat

Output:

Curve Status: STALLED
Suggested action: avoid new entries / consider exit
11. Exit risk alerts

Since you are trading before migration, you need alerts before the chart turns ugly.

Alerts:

- Buy/sell ratio flipped negative
- Curve progress stalled
- 5m volume down 50% from prior 5m
- Unique buyers stopped increasing
- Large holder sent tokens to another wallet
- Token dropped below previous bonding zone support
